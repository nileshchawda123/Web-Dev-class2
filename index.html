Virat Kohli opens up on playing cricket in USA ahead of T20 World Cup: ‘Starting of a domino effect’

India batting maestro Kohli talked about playing in the USA and said he never thought that he would be playing in the States The stage is set for the T20 World Cup 2024 as the USA is all set to co-host the tournament alongside the West Indies. The mega ICC event is going to be extremely crucial to create massive awareness about the sport in the USA where basketball and baseball are enjoyed by a wider audience. Big players like Virat Kohli will also play a big role in promoting the game in the states as cricket will also be part of the Los Angeles Olympics in 2028.
